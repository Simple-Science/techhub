Product Details

The Samsung Galaxy Tab S9 Ultra is a powerful tablet designed to meet your productivity and entertainment needs. With its sleek design and impressive features, it’s a versatile companion. The tablet features a 14.6-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with a 120Hz refresh rate and HDR10+ support, ensuring vibrant visuals and smooth interactions. Powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 chipset, it delivers smooth multitasking with 12GB of RAM and storage options of 256GB or 512GB (expandable via microSDXC). Capture moments with the dual 13 MP + 8 MP rear camera, and enjoy selfies with the dual 12 MP front camera. The tablet houses a non-removable 11,200 mAh Li-Po battery that supports 45W wired charging. It features a glass front, aluminum frame, and aluminum back, and is water-resistant (IP68). Stylus support with low latency enhances productivity. Whether you’re working, streaming, or creating, the Samsung Galaxy Tab S9 Ultra has you covered
