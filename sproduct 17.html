Product Details

The Surface Pro 9 is a versatile 2-in-1 laptop-tablet hybrid designed by Microsoft. It features a 13-inch PixelSense display with a resolution of 2880 x 1920 pixels, providing crisp visuals and touch functionality. Powered by the 11th Gen Intel Core processors, it offers robust performance for productivity tasks, creative work, and entertainment. With options for 8GB or 16GB of RAM and 128GB to 1TB of SSD storage, users can tailor it to their needs. The sleek design, lightweight build, and support for the Surface Pen make it an excellent choice for professionals, students, and anyone seeking a portable computing solution.
