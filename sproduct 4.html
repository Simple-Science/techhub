Apple Watch Ultra 2

RM 3,799.00

Product Details

The Apple Watch Ultra 2 is a rugged and capable smartwatch designed for those seeking top-tier features. It features a 1.92-inch Retina LTPO OLED display with a resolution of 502 x 410 pixels and support for ProMotion technology with adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz. The watch is powered by the S9 SiP with a 64-bit dual-core processor and a 4-core Apple Neural Engine. It offers 64GB capacity for storage. Health sensors include an electrical heart sensor, ECG app, third-generation optical heart sensor, and temperature sensing. The watch is water-resistant up to 100 meters and tested against various environmental conditions. Connectivity options include Precision dual-frequency GPS, LTE, Wi-Fi 4 (802.11n), and Bluetooth 5.3. The built-in rechargeable lithium-ion battery provides up to 36 hours of normal use and up to 72 hours in Low Power Mode. Whether you’re tracking fitness, receiving notifications, or exploring the outdoors, the Apple Watch Ultra 2 delivers a comprehensive experience
