MSI Thin GF63 11UC 886

RM 3,899

Product Details

The MSI GF63 Thin 11UC-886 is a gaming laptop that strikes a balance between performance and portability. It features a 15.6-inch Full HD (1920x1080) IPS-level panel with a 144Hz refresh rate for smooth visuals during gaming. Powered by an 11th Gen. Intel® Core™ i7-11800H processor, it delivers robust performance for multitasking and gaming. Equipped with an NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 3050 Laptop GPU (4GB GDDR6), it ensures impressive graphics performance. Comes with 8GB DDR4 RAM (expandable) and a 512GB NVMe PCIe Gen3x4 SSD for fast storage access. The laptop features an aluminum hairbrush aesthetics with an asymmetric pattern, making it visually appealing. It’s also slim at 21.7mm and lightweight at 1.86kg. Supports Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth v5.2 for seamless wireless connectivity. Features a single red-backlit keyboard for comfortable typing even in low-light conditions. Equipped with a 3-cell, 51Whr battery for decent on-the-go usage.
