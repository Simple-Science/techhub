Product Details

The Apple MacBook Pro M2 Pro is a high-performance laptop designed for professionals and power users. It features a 14-inch 3.5K OLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, delivering stunning visuals and vibrant colors. With 16GB unified memory and a 512GB SSD, it ensures smooth multitasking and fast storage access. The laptop also includes features like the Touch Bar, Touch ID, and a studio-quality three-mic array. Whether you’re editing videos, designing graphics, or running resource-intensive applications, the MacBook Pro M2 Pro delivers exceptional performance
