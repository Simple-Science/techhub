Product Details

The Samsung Galaxy Tab S9+ is a powerful tablet designed to meet your productivity and entertainment needs. With its sleek design and impressive features, it’s a versatile companion. The tablet boasts an 11.0-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with a 120Hz refresh rate and HDR10+ support. Whether you’re working on documents or streaming content, the vibrant visuals enhance your experience. Powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 chipset, the Tab S9+ delivers smooth multitasking. It comes with 8GB or 12GB of RAM and 128GB or 256GB of internal storage (expandable via microSDXC). Capture moments with the 13 MP rear camera featuring LED flash and HDR. The 12 MP front camera with a wide-angle lens ensures great selfies and video calls.The tablet houses a non-removable 8,400 mAh Li-Po battery that supports 45W wired charging. Stay productive and entertained without worrying about battery life. Enjoy Wi-Fi 6E for fast internet connectivity. Whether you’re working, streaming, or gaming, the Samsung Galaxy Tab S9+ has you covered.
