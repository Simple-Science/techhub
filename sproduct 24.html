Product Details

Certainly! The iPad Pro 12.9-inch is a powerhouse tablet that combines performance, stunning display, and versatility. Featuring a 12.9-inch Liquid Retina XDR display, it offers a resolution of 2732 x 2048 pixels with ProMotion technology, wide color support, and True Tone. The display is both bright (up to 600 nits in SDR mode) and HDR-ready (up to 1600 nits peak for HDR content). Powered by the Apple M2 chip, it boasts an 8-core CPU, 10-core GPU, and a 16-core Neural Engine. Models come with either 8GB or 16GB of RAM and storage options ranging from 128GB to 2TB. The dual-camera system includes a 12 MP Wide camera and a 10 MP Ultra Wide camera. It supports features like Smart HDR 3, 2x optical zoom out, and 4K video recording. With a sleek aluminum body, it weighs 1.5 pounds and features a USB-C port, Smart Connector, and support for the Apple Pencil (2nd generation).
