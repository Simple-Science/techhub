Product Details

The Iphone 14 pro max is a flagship smartphone from Apple, offering top-tier features and performance. It features a 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with a resolution of 2796 x 1290 pixels and support for ProMotion technology with adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz. The phone is powered by the A17 Pro chip, which includes a new 6-core CPU, 6-core GPU, and a 16-core Neural Engine. The camera system includes a 48MP main camera with sensor-shift optical image stabilization, an ultra-wide 12MP camera, and a 5x telephoto lens. The device is rated IP68 water and dust resistant and supports 5G connectivity. With its sleek design, powerful performance, and advanced camera capabilities, the Iphone 14 pro max is a compelling choice for users seeking a premium smartphone experience
