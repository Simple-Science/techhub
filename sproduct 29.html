Lenovo Tab P12 Tablet

RM 1,999.00

Product Details

The Lenovo Tab P12 tablet boasts a 12.7-inch IPS LCD display with a resolution of 1840 x 2944 pixels and an aspect ratio of 16:10. It is powered by the Mediatek Dimensity 7050 chipset, which includes an octa-core CPU (2x2.6 GHz Cortex-A78 and 6x2.0 GHz Cortex-A55) and a Mali-G68 MC4 GPU. The tablet comes in two RAM configurations: 4GB or 8GB, with internal storage options of 128GB (4GB RAM), 128GB (8GB RAM), and 256GB (8GB RAM). The main camera is an 8 MP wide-angle lens, while the selfie camera is a 13 MP ultrawide camera. The non-removable 10,200 mAh Li-Po battery supports 30W wired charging. Connectivity features include Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.1, and a USB Type-C 2.0 port. The tablet measures 293.3 x 190.7 x 6.9 mm and weighs 615 grams. Stylus support is included, and it’s available in Storm Grey and Oat colors .
