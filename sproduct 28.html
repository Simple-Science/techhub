Product Details

The Xiaomi Pad 6 is a versatile tablet designed to enhance both productivity and entertainment. Its 11-inch WQHD+ display with a 144Hz refresh rate ensures vibrant visuals and smooth interactions. The slim metal unibody design comes in three elegant colors: Gravity Gray, Mist Blue, and Gold. Under the hood, the tablet is powered by the Snapdragon® 870 Mobile Platform, offering exceptional performance. With 6GB of RAM and 128GB of storage, you’ll have ample space for your files and apps. The tablet’s 8,840mAh battery supports 33W fast charging, providing up to 16 hours of video playback. Plus, the quad speakers with Dolby Atmos® deliver immersive sound. Whether you’re working or unwinding, the Xiaomi Pad 6 has you covered .
